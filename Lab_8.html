Results

Figure 1 demonstrates the relationship between teacher experience and students math scale scores. The two regression lines demonstrate differences between free and reduced price lunch status, which students who are not eligible for free and reduced price lunch scoring, in general, higher than those who do. In general, it does not appear that teacher experience significantly impacts this difference, as math scores for students both with and without free and reduced price lunch status appears to increase at approximately the same rate.
